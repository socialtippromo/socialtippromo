The Problem

Issues where value leaks, a plan to resolve them and a proposal for growth.

Initial problems identified from an app walkthrough, your public help docs, online research and platform constraints for Instagram & TikTok.

Onboarding: Sign up with very little guidance, means the “what now?” effect is high.
Instagram connect: two paths (personal via DM; Professional via OAuth) with too much context‑switching.
Brand discovery: Search will fail with limited brands; brand pages when accessed through a different flow lack an obvious 'Make a post' CTA.
Posting: rules across several screens creates a memory burden so it is easy to miss steps.
Earnings & payouts: Cadence, process and ETA aren’t surfaced in‑app.

Social Tip shows strong demand signals on both sides of the marketplace. The fastest path to compounding growth is to (a) reduce creator activation friction; (b) make verification & payouts faster and less manual; and (c) give brands a clear, self‑serve ROI loop. The plan below sets out accountable verticals, quantifiable KPIs, and experiments you can ship quickly.

Product Verticals

Each area needs it's own vision, strategy and KPIs that align with Social Tip's objectives.

As Product Leader, my role would be to ensure each vertical's strategic goals are aligned with the business objectives, researched thoroughly in terms of highest ROI either from a revenue or brand/market perspective, prioritised ruthlessly and KPIs tracked.

Creator Acquisition & Activation

Scope: sign‑up → connect → first post → first payout; education, nudges, re‑activation.

KPIs: activation rate and retention, time‑to‑first‑post, D7 retention, first‑payout rate.

Social Integrations (IG/TikTok)

Scope: OAuth/permissions, token health, post/insights matching.

KPIs: link success, token refresh success, auto‑match rate, % auto‑approved posts.

iOS & Android Consumer App

Scope: onboarding, brand discovery, posting checklist, receipts, support.

KPIs: task completion rate, error rate, app store CSAT, crash‑free sessions.

Brand Portal & Campaigns

Scope: brand onboarding, budgets, campaigns, teammates, ROI dashboards.

KPIs: time‑to‑first‑campaign, budget utilisation, cost per rewarded post, weekly active admins.

Payments, Wallet & Finance Ops

Scope: deposits, ledgers, creator payouts, reconciliation.

KPIs: payout success rate, payout latency, chargebacks, finance ops time.

Trust, Safety & Policy

Scope: disclosure enforcement, negative‑post handling, brand safety, fraud checks.

KPIs: compliance rate, moderation SLA, dispute rate, fraud‑free payout rate.

The Plan

A focused, outcomes based roadmap.

Based on multiple assumptions, including the business goals of driving growth through reduced consumer friction and increased value for Brands.

Now

Outcome: Implement app analytics to track funnel drop‑off and conversion rates.
Measures: baseline conversion per step; time‑to‑first‑post; verification tracking; payout time.
What ships: event taxonomy & reporting; error codes etc.
Owner(s): Creator Acquisition & Activation (analytics), Payments (timers).

Measures: ↑ link success; ↓ time‑to‑first‑post; ↑ post‑start → proof‑submit rate (targets set from baseline analytics).

Quick fixes for Day One

UX and Design choices which will have an immediate impact.

Onboarding

3-card first-timer explainer

On first open, show a three-card tour that maps Connect → Pick a brand → Post, prove, payout, then pin a simple checklist to Home. Short, skippable tips reduce confusion, keep users on one path, and make the “what happens next” totally clear.

Brand Flow

'Make a post' CTA visibility & search

Put a primary Make a post button on every brand page, and upgrade search with instant autocomplete, typo-tolerance, and a clear Request a brand fallback. This removes dead ends, shortens time to action, and turns browsing into posting.

Profile

Earnings & stats

Provide a one-tap caption builder with locked #Ad and required mentions, live checks for tags, and a Copy caption button. Add a simple earnings preview (per post/week/month) so value is obvious upfront and users don’t guess what to write or earn.

Consumer expectation

Payout timeline component

Add a small status widget that says “Next payout: Friday • ETA: 2–3 business days” and updates as a claim moves from submitted → verified → paid. Clear states set expectations, reduce “when do I get paid?” tickets, and build trust to post again.
